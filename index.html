IT Proffession

IT professionals are individuals who test, develop, install, repair, and maintain the hardware and software associated with sophisticated computer systems located in one or more locations. Learning what IT professionals do can help you decide if it's the right career path for you. Information technology professionals study, design, develop, implement, support, and manage computer-based information systems, including both software applications and computer hardware. Current top IT skills: Data science, Programming languages, Cloud computing, AI & Machine learning, and Software development. Having these key skills will help you stay competitive in the job market, unlock higher salaries and succeed in your field.
